❓ FAQ

How often do you tally votes?

I count votes weekly and announce the top 1-2 most requested artists/shows. Then I feature them in my next videos!

Can I vote multiple times?

You can submit multiple requests, but each submission counts as one vote. So if you want me to react to a certain artist, you can submit multiple times!

Do Patreon supporters get priority?

Yes! Patreon members get extra votes (their votes count as 3x), so their requests count more. It's a small way to thank supporters while keeping all content free for everyone.

What if my request doesn't win?

Don't worry! Top requests from every week get cycled through. If you don't win this week, your artist could be featured next week or in a future "Viewer Requests" video.
